.. - obaznachaet predidushiyu direktoriyu

. -obaznachaet tekushuyu direktoriyu
1. ls(list) -> vyvodit spisok vsex direktorii i failov v tekushiy direktoriy (rabochaya direktoriya)

ls -a -> vyvodit spisok kak i skrytyh tak i obychnyh failov i direktorii 

ls -l -> vyvodit spisok direktorii i failov s podrobnoy informasiey 

2. cd ( change directory) -> menyat tekushiyu direktoriyu na ukazannuyu

cd < nazvaniya direktorii>

3. mkdir(make direktory) -> Sozdayot ukazannuyu direktoriyu 
mkdir < direktoriya>

4. touch -> sozdayot ukazanuy fail 
touch <name_of_file>.<extension(rasshirenie(txt, docs))>
mv
5.pwd(print working directory) -> vyvodit put do tekushey direktory
ctrl+shift+c  kopiruet terminale 

ctrl+shift+v  vstavit v terminale 

6) rmdir ( remove directory)  -> udalyaet ukazannuyu direktoriyu(ne mojet udalit ne pustuyu  direktoriyu) 

7. rm -> udalenie failov 
rm <name_of_file>

rm -rf -> prinuditelnoe udalenie faila libo direktorii 

8. mv (move)-> peremeshaet libo pereimenovyet fail libo direktoriyu, v zavisimosti ot ukazannovo vtorovo argumenta 

mv <fail_direktorya> <novoe_imya -- direktoriya/>

9.cat -> vyvodit soderjimoe ukazannogo faila

10. nano -> otkryvaet graficheskiy interfeis v terminale dlya redaktirovanie faila
*esli net ukazannogo faila , to sozdatsa novyi fail s takim imenem 

nano <name_of_fail>

11. echo -> komanda dlya bystroy zapisi dannyh v fail cherez terminal

echo <text dlya zapisi> nazvanie faila


12. cp (copy) - komanda dlya kopirovaniya faila v nujnuyu direktoriyu 

cp <file_to_copy> <path>
